The Story of Minerva Tattoo Studio

I started Minerva Tattoo in May of 2006 with the idea that I wanted to help people express themselves through body art. I decided to find the best artists in Denver to create a truly unique experience for all of our clients, and over 15 years later, I feel like we have a studio like no other.

At Minerva, we want everyone's tattoo to create a story about who they are and what they represent. Our artists are passionate, world-class talents that make our shop the first choice for people in the Denver metro area, and our studio is a clean and comfortable environment that projects who we are and what we do. From specialty custom work, to smaller projects, let us create something special for you.
